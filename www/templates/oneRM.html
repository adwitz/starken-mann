Let's find your 1RM!
The key is to start with lighter weights / higher reps and then work your way to higher weights / lower reps
1. Do two sets of 4-5 reps with a light weight. Rest two minutes.
2. Increase weight to one you can lift roughly eight times. Rest three minutes.
3. Increase weight to one you can lift three-four times. Rest three minutes.
4. Increase weight to approxmate 1RM. And try to do one rep.
-If it was too easy rest three minutes, increase weight and repeat.
-If you could not perform one rep, rest three minutes and decrease weight
Note: for the purposes of this application, your 1RM should be between 100 and 580 lbs and divisible by 5.
